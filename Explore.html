Pencils


Sratch: For beginners any company's graphite pencils can be used. At start you should draw with single pencil.

Advanced: For making your drawing look more attractive graphite pencils with different grading scale is prefeered. Pencils are graded as:9H,8H, 7H, 6H, 5H, 4H, 3H, 2H, H, F, HB, B, 2B, 3B, 4B, 5B, 6B, 7B,8B, 9B. Stardard sets usually run from 2H to 6B grades using this grades pencils can cover most of the techniques. Here, you can see grades as H or B. The grade H stands for hard, B for blackness and F for fine point.

Paper

Scratch:Beginners can use normal blank sheets or drawing book and for practice you can also make use low quality paper and as you achieve your goal you can then start with high quality paper.

Advanced: For catchy sketch cambridge paper, ivory paper is used.



Blending Stumps


Sratch:New learners can start blending using ear buds, tisuue paper and rough cotton cloth. It is not necessary to use blending stump alternative option will also work.

Advanced:Learners can use the same alternative as mentioned before and can also make use of blending stumps or tortillon.
