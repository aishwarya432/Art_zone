HOW TO START?


Many of you have drawing as an hobby and don't know how to get started and make best of your skills. First thing is to take initiative an start learning, any kind of art is a skill it depends on your practice and passion towards it. More you practice better you get.

For creating a perfect sketch start with CARTOONS and for making good drawings, draw simple and easy objects. Achieving your long-term goals always starts with completion of short-term goals.
